About me

2023-05-10

My name is Shiyao(Giselle) Long, and I am originally from Wuhan, China. I am currently a graduate student at the Hong Kong University of Science and Technology, studying Information Technology. Prior to that, I completed my undergraduate studies in Computer Science at Queen's University in Canada.

Throughout my academic journey, I have developed a keen interest in product management. I am passionate about creating user-centered products that not only solve real-world problems but also enhance the user's experience. I believe that effective communication and collaboration are crucial skills for a product manager, and I strive to continuously improve in these areas.

In my free time, I enjoy hiking, trying new cuisines, and learning about different cultures.
