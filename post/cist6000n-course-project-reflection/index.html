CIST6000N Course Project Reflection

2023-05-10

What have I done

For the first phase of the project, I contributed in the storyboard construction and built up the whole story. I also contributed in design the questionaire. And for the second phase of the project, Huang Qiuhua and I designed the user interface of our fitness coaching app, and produced the prototype. And I also filmed the video demo and edited it. For both of the phases, I am responsible for producing high quality slides.

My reflection

Designing this fitness coaching app has been an excellent opportunity for me to apply the principles of human-computer interaction in a real-world context. Throughout the design process, I had to consider not only the functionality of the app but also how it would interact with the user in a meaningful and engaging way. I found that the key principles of user-centered design, such as customization, simplicity, and consistency, were critical in creating a user-friendly and enjoyable experience for our users. Additionally, the integration of AI and computer vision technology allowed us to create innovative features that enhance the user's workout experience in a personalized and interactive way. Overall, this project has reinforced my understanding of the importance of user-centered design and the potential of technology to create more engaging and meaningful interactions between humans and computers.
